The story

Why YAARD?

"Yaard" — the Jamaican patois word for home, for yard, for the place you belong. We chose it because this platform is meant to be exactly that: a home for Caribbean creatives to express themselves online without having to borrow aesthetics that were never designed for them.

Pinterest data shows that searches like "melanin pfp," "Caribbean aesthetic profile pic," and "dark skin aesthetic portrait" are rising fast — yet there's virtually no Caribbean-specific content serving this audience. YAARD fills that gap with real, culturally grounded styling guides powered by AI, so that every 18-to-35-year-old in Jamaica, Trinidad, Barbados, or anywhere in the diaspora can create a profile picture that truly represents who they are.

We're just getting started. More aesthetics, more Caribbean-specific content, and more tools are coming. Follow our Pinterest to stay updated.
